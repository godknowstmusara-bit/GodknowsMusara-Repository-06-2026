The Lion - King of the Jungle

The lion is one of the most famous animals in the world. Lions are known for their strength, courage, and social behavior. They live in groups called prides and are mainly found in Africa. Male lions are recognized by their manes, while female lions do most of the hunting. Lions help maintain balance in nature and are an important part of the ecosystem. Unfortunately, habitat loss and human activities have reduced lion populations. Conservation efforts are helping to protect these magnificent animals for future generations.

Interesting Facts About Lions

  • Lions live in groups called prides.
  • A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 kilometers away.
  • Female lions do most of the hunting.
  • Lions can sleep up to 20 hours a day.
